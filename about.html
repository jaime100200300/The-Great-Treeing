The Great Treeing

The Great Treeing is a wonderful event where the trees… they tree. They don’t grow, they don’t sprout, they don’t bloom, and they certainly don’t photosynthesize politely.

No. They TREE.

They tree with intention.
They tree with velocity.
They tree with the raw, unfiltered power of chlorophyll‑based determination.

It is said that during this event, birds pause mid‑chirp, rivers hesitate mid‑flow, even the wind whispers dang, and clouds drift respectfully.
