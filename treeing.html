The Lore of the Treeing

The Great Treeing is not merely an event — it is a phenomenon. A moment where the trees do not simply grow… they TREE.

Legends say that during the Treeing, birds pause mid‑chirp, rivers hesitate mid‑flow, and even the wind whispers “dang.”
